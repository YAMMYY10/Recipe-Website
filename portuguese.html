PORTUGUESE CUISINE


Bacalhau à Gomes de Sá

Portugal is famous for its salt cod, or bacalhau. Bacalhau à Gomes de Sá, which is a casserole made with salt cod, potatoes, onions, and olives, and Bacalhau com Natas, which is a creamy gratin made with salt cod, potatoes, and a bechamel sauce.




Cozido à Portuguesa

This is a hearty stew that includes a variety of meats, such as beef, pork, and chicken, as well as vegetables, beans, and potatoes. It's a popular dish during the winter months and is often served with rice.




Sardinhas Assadas

Sardinhas Assadas, or grilled sardines consists of fresh sardines that are cleaned, seasoned with salt and pepper, and grilled over an open flame until they are golden and crispy on the outside and tender and juicy on the inside.




Arroz de Pato

Arroz de Pato is a traditional Portuguese dish that translates to "duck rice." It's a hearty casserole-style dish made with tender duck meat, rice, onions, garlic, and a variety of spices and seasonings, such as bay leaves, paprika, and thyme.




Pasteis de Nata

These creamy custard tarts are a must-try when visiting Portugal. They're made with a crispy puff pastry shell and a rich, creamy custard filling, and are often dusted with cinnamon and powdered sugar.
