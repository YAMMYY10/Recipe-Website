TARAMOSALATA

INGREDIENTS



  • 200g tarama (fish roe)
  • 2-3 slices of stale bread
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Water (optional)


INSTRUCTIONS



  • Soak the bread in water for a few minutes until it becomes soft. Squeeze out the excess water and set aside.
  • In a blender or food processor, add the tarama, onion, and garlic. Blend until smooth.
  • Slowly pour in the olive oil and lemon juice while blending, until the mixture is fully combined.
  • Add the soaked bread to the mixture and blend again until smooth.
  • Taste and season with salt and pepper as needed. If the mixture is too thick, add a little water until it reaches the desired consistency.
  • Serve chilled with pita bread, vegetables, or crackers.
