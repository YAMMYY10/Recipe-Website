SUSHI

INGREDIENTS



  • Sushi rice
  • Nori sheets
  • Sushi vinegar
  • Assorted fillings (e.g. cucumber,
    avocado, crab, shrimp, tuna, salmon)
  • Soy sauce
  • Wasabi
  • Pickled ginger


INSTRUCTIONS



  • Rinse the sushi rice in cold water until the water runs clear.
  • Add the rice and water to a rice cooker or pot and cook according to package instructions.
  • Once the rice is cooked, transfer it to a large mixing bowl.
  • Add sushi vinegar to the rice and mix gently to coat all the grains evenly. Let the rice cool to room temperature.
  • Cut the fillings into thin strips or bite-sized pieces.
  • Lay a sheet of nori on a sushi mat, shiny side down.
  • Wet your hands with water and grab a handful of rice. Place it on the nori sheet and spread it out evenly,
    leaving a 1-inch border at the top.
  • Arrange the fillings on top of the rice, making sure to distribute them evenly.
  • Roll the sushi tightly using the sushi mat, tucking the filling in as you go.
  • Use a sharp knife to cut the sushi roll into bite-sized pieces.
  • Serve the sushi with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger on the side.
  • Enjoy your homemade Japanese sushi!
