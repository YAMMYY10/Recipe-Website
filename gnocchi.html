GNOCCHI DI PATATE

INGREDIENTS



  • 2 lbs potatoes, peeled and boiled
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley


INSTRUCTIONS



  • Peel and boil the potatoes until they are tender. Drain them and let them cool for a few minutes.
  • Pass the boiled potatoes through a potato ricer or a sieve and into a large mixing bowl.
  • Add the flour, egg, salt, and pepper to the bowl with the potatoes. Mix everything together to form a smooth dough.
  • Divide the dough into 4 equal pieces. Roll each piece into a long rope about 1/2 inch thick. Cut the ropes into 1 inch pieces.
  • Use a fork to make indentations on each gnocchi. This will help the sauce stick to the gnocchi.
  • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the gnocchi and cook for 2-3 minutes or until they float to the surface.
  • In a separate pan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the chopped parsley and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  • Drain the gnocchi and add them to the pan with the butter and parsley. Toss everything together until the gnocchi are coated with the butter and parsley sauce.
  • Serve the gnocchi hot, topped with freshly grated Parmesan cheese.
