PISTO

INGREDIENTS



  • 2 medium-sized eggplants, chopped
  • 2 medium-sized zucchinis, chopped
  • 2 red bell peppers, chopped
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 large ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley


INSTRUCTIONS



  • Heat the olive oil in a large frying pan over medium heat.
  • Add the chopped eggplants, zucchinis, red bell peppers, onions, and garlic to the pan and cook, stirring occasionally, for 10-15 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender and lightly browned.
  • Add the chopped tomatoes to the pan and cook for an additional 5-10 minutes, or until the tomatoes have broken down and the mixture has thickened.
  • Season the pisto with salt and black pepper to taste.
  • Garnish the pisto with chopped fresh parsley before serving.
  • Enjoy as a side dish or as a topping for bread or rice.
