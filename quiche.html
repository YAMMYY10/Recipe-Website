QUICHE LORRAINE

INGREDIENTS



  • 1 pie crust, homemade or store-bought
  • 200g bacon, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 eggs
  • 250ml heavy cream
  • 100g grated Gruyere cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Olive oil
  • Butter


INSTRUCTIONS



  • Preheat the oven to 190Â°C.
  • Roll out the pie crust and press it into a 9-inch pie dish. Chill the crust in the refrigerator while you prepare the filling.
  • Heat some olive oil and butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  • Add the bacon and onion to the skillet and cook until the bacon is crispy and the onion is softened.
  • Remove the skillet from the heat and let the bacon and onion mixture cool for a few minutes.
  • In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs and heavy cream.
  • Add the bacon and onion mixture to the bowl and stir everything together.
  • Stir in the grated Gruyere cheese, and season the mixture with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Pour the filling into the chilled pie crust.
  • Bake the quiche for 35-40 minutes, or until the filling is set and the crust is golden brown.
  • Remove the quiche from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
  • Enjoy your delicious Quiche Lorraine!
