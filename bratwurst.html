BRATWURST

INGREDIENTS



  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried marjoram
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 cup cold water
  • Pork casings (optional)


INSTRUCTIONS



  • In a large bowl, combine the ground pork, ground beef, finely chopped onion, minced garlic, paprika,
    salt, black pepper, dried thyme, dried marjoram, ground coriander, ground allspice, ground nutmeg,
    cayenne pepper, and cold water.
  • Using your hands, mix the ingredients together until well combined.
  • If using pork casings, soak them in warm water for at least 30 minutes before using.
  • Fit a sausage stuffer with the sausage-making attachment and fill it with the meat mixture.
  • Thread a length of pork casing onto the stuffer tube and start feeding the meat mixture into the casing, twisting it periodically to create links.
  • Continue stuffing the casing until all the meat mixture is used up.
  • If not using casings, form the meat mixture into patties or meatballs.
  • Heat a large skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  • Once hot, add the bratwurst links (or patties/meatballs) to the pan and cook, turning occasionally,
    until browned on all sides and cooked through, about 10-12 minutes.
  • Transfer the bratwurst links to a platter and let rest for a few minutes before serving.
  • Serve and enjoy!.
