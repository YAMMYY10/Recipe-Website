CHINESE CUISINE


Ma Po Tofu

Ma Po Tofu is a classic Sichuan Chinese dish that typically consists of tofu, minced pork, fermented black beans, and a spicy sauce made with chili bean paste, Sichuan peppercorns, and other seasonings.




Chinese Dumplings

Chinese dumplings, also known as jiaozi, are a popular type of traditional Chinese food. They consist of a filling, usually made of minced meat, vegetables, and seasoning, wrapped in a thin dough wrapper.




Kung Pao Chicken

Kung Pao Chicken is made with diced chicken that is stir-fried with peanuts, vegetables, and chili peppers, and then coated in a savory sauce of soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, and a combination of spices such as Sichuan peppercorns, ginger, and garlic.




Peking Roasted Duck

Peking Roast Duck, also known as Beijing Roast Duck, is a famous Chinese dish that is marinated in a combination of Chinese five-spice powder, soy sauce, and other seasonings, hung to dry and then roasted in a closed oven to give it golden brown texture.




Zhajiangmian

Zhajiangmian, also known as "noodles with soybean paste", is a popular Chinese dish made with wheat noodles that are topped with a sauce made from fermented soybean paste (known as "doubanjiang"), ground pork or beef, and vegetables.
