TORTILLA ESPANOLA

INGREDIENTS



  • 4 medium-sized potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 medium-sized onion, thinly sliced
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper


INSTRUCTIONS



  • Heat the olive oil in a large non-stick frying pan over medium heat.
  • Add the sliced potatoes and onion to the pan and cook, stirring occasionally, until the potatoes are tender and lightly browned, about 20 minutes.
  • Remove the potatoes and onion from the pan using a slotted spoon and set them aside to cool slightly.
  • In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, salt, and black pepper until well-combined.
  • Add the cooled potatoes and onion to the egg mixture and stir gently to combine.
  • Pour the egg and potato mixture back into the frying pan and cook over low heat for 10-15 minutes, or until the bottom is golden brown and set.
  • To flip the tortilla, place a large plate over the top of the frying pan and carefully invert the tortilla onto the plate.
  • Slide the tortilla back into the frying pan and cook for an additional 5-10 minutes, or until the top is set and lightly golden.
  • Remove the tortilla from the heat and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing it into wedges.
  • Serve and enjoy!.
