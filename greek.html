GREEK CUISINE


Moussaka

Moussaka is a traditional dish made with layers of eggplant, ground meat (usually beef or lamb), tomatoes, and onions, topped with a creamy b√©chamel sauce and baked in the oven.




Papoutsakia

Papoutsakia is a Greek dish that consists of roasted eggplants that are hollowed out and filled with a meat sauce made with ground beef or lamb, onions, garlic, tomatoes, and spices like cinnamon and allspice.




Pastitsio

Pastitsio is made with layers of long, hollow pasta tubes called ziti or bucatini, topped with a meat sauce made with ground beef or lamb, onions, garlic, and tomatoes.




Taramosalata

Taramosalata is a traditional Greek dip that is made from tarama, which is salted and cured fish roe, typically from cod or carp typically served as an appetizer with bread or crackers.




Souvlaki

Souvlaki (or sometimes spelled as "souvlakia") is a popular Greek dish made of small pieces of meat, typically pork, chicken, or lamb, that are skewered and grilled. The meat is often marinated in a combination of olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and herbs before being cooked.
