DULCE DE LECHA

INGREDIENTS



  • 2 liters of whole milk
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 vanilla bean, split lengthwise (optional)


INSTRUCTIONS



  • Pour the milk into a large, heavy-bottomed pot and heat over medium heat until it reaches a simmer.
  • Add the sugar, baking soda, and vanilla bean (if using) and stir to combine.
  • Reduce the heat to low and let the mixture simmer, stirring occasionally, for about 2 hours or until
    it has thickened and turned a caramel color.
  • Remove the pot from the heat and let the mixture cool for a few minutes.
  • Remove the vanilla bean (if using) and discard.
  • Use an immersion blender or transfer the mixture to a blender and puree until smooth.
  • Transfer the dulce de leche to a container with a tight-fitting lid and let it cool to room temperature.
  • Refrigerate the dulce de leche for at least 2 hours or until it has set up and thickened to a spreadable consistency.
  • Use the dulce de leche as a spread or topping for bread, pastries, or other desserts.
