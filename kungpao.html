KUNG PAO CHICKEN

INGREDIENTS



  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs, cut into small pieces
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp Shaoxing wine (or dry sherry)
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup roasted peanuts
  • 4-5 dried red chili peppers
  • 1 tbsp Szechuan peppercorns
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth


INSTRUCTIONS



  • In a bowl, combine the chicken, soy sauce, Shaoxing wine, and cornstarch. Stir well to coat the chicken evenly. Let it marinate for at least 15 minutes.
  • Heat the vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat. When the oil is hot, add the chicken and
    stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until browned and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the wok and
    set it aside.
  • Lower the heat to medium and add the roasted peanuts, dried chili peppers, and Szechuan peppercorns
    to the wok. Stir-fry for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  • Add the garlic, ginger, and green onions to the wok and stir-fry for another minute.
  • In a small bowl, mix together the hoisin sauce, soy sauce, rice vinegar, sugar, and chicken broth. Pour
    the sauce into the wok and stir well to combine.
  • Add the cooked chicken back into the wok and stir-fry for another 1-2 minutes until everything is
    coated in the sauce and heated through.
  • Serve hot with steamed rice.
  • Enjoy your delicious Kung Pao Chicken!
