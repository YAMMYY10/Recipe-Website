ONIGIRI

INGREDIENTS



  • Sushi rice
  • Nori sheets (optional)
  • Assorted fillings (e.g. pickled plum, canned tuna,
    cooked salmon, cooked chicken, grilled vegetables)
  • Salt
  • Water


INSTRUCTIONS



  • Rinse the sushi rice in cold water until the water runs clear.
  • Add the rice and water to a rice cooker or pot and cook according to package instructions.
  • Once the rice is cooked, transfer it to a large mixing bowl.
  • Add salt to the rice and mix gently to season evenly.
  • Let the rice cool to room temperature.
  • Wet your hands with water and grab a handful of rice.
  • Flatten the rice in the palm of your hand and make an indentation in the center.
  • Add a small amount of filling to the indentation.
  • Fold the rice around the filling and shape it into a triangle or ball.
  • If desired, wrap a strip of nori around the onigiri.
  • Repeat with the remaining rice and filling.
  • Serve the onigiri at room temperature or chilled.
  • Enjoy your homemade Japanese onigiri!
