BUTTER CHICKEN

INGREDIENTS



  • 1.5 lbs boneless chicken, cut into small pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger paste
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter, cubed
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish


INSTRUCTIONS



  • In a large bowl, whisk together the yogurt, cumin, coriander, turmeric, paprika, garam masala, cayenne pepper, and salt.
  • Add the chicken and mix until well coated. Cover and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour
    or overnight.
  • Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add the garlic and ginger paste and cook for another minute.
  • Add the chicken to the skillet and cook until browned on all sides, about 8-10 minutes. Pour in the tomato puree
    and stir to combine.
  • Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
  • Stir in the heavy cream and butter until well combined. Simmer for another 5 minutes.
  • Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with rice or naan bread.
