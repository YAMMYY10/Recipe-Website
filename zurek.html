ZUREK

INGREDIENTS



  • 1 lb sourdough rye bread
  • 8 cups water
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp caraway seeds
  • 1 lb kielbasa sausage, sliced
  • 1 cup cooked white sausage
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and black pepper to taste


INSTRUCTIONS



  • Cut the bread into small cubes and place them in a large pot.
  • Add the water, onion, garlic, bay leaves, and caraway seeds to the pot and bring the mixture to a boil.
  • Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 1-2 hours, or until the bread is fully dissolved and the soup is thickened.
  • Remove the bay leaves from the pot.
  • Add the sliced kielbasa and cooked white sausage to the pot and simmer for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the sausage is fully cooked.
  • In a small bowl, mix together the sour cream, flour, and egg yolk until smooth.
  • Slowly add a ladleful of hot soup to the sour cream mixture, whisking constantly, to temper the mixture.
  • Add the tempered sour cream mixture to the pot and stir well.
  • Simmer for an additional 5-10 minutes, or until the soup has thickened and the flavors have melded together.
  • Season the soup with salt and black pepper to taste.
  • Serve hot, garnished with chopped parsley.
  • Enjoy your homemade Polish Zurek!
