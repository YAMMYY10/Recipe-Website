BANH XEO

INGREDIENTS



    For the batter:

  • 1 cup of rice flour
  • 1/2 cup of coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1/2 tsp. of turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp. of salt
  • 1 green onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil

    • For the filling:

  • 1/2 lb. of pork shoulder, thinly
    sliced
  • 1/2 lb. of shrimp, peeled and
    deveined
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup of bean sprouts
  • 1/2 cup of fresh herbs (such as
    mint and cilantro)
  • Lettuce leaves, for serving
  • Dipping sauce (such as nuoc
    cham), for serving


INSTRUCTIONS



  • In a large bowl, whisk together the rice flour, coconut milk, water, turmeric powder, salt, and sliced
    green onion to make the batter. Let the batter rest for 30 minutes.
  • In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the sliced pork
    shoulder and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until browned and cooked through. Transfer to a plate and set aside.
  • In the same skillet, add the shrimp and stir-fry for 1-2 minutes until pink and cooked through.
    Transfer to a plate and set aside.
  • Using the same skillet, add another tablespoon of vegetable oil and sauté the sliced onion until
    softened and slightly caramelized. Add the bean sprouts and stir-fry for 1-2 minutes until heated through.
    Transfer to a plate and set aside.
  • Heat a 10-inch non-stick skillet over medium-high heat. Add 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil and swirl
    to coat the bottom of the skillet. Pour in 1/2 cup of the batter and quickly swirl the skillet to spread the
    batter evenly. Cover the skillet and cook for 1-2 minutes until the bottom of the banh xeo is crispy
    and golden brown.
  • Place a few slices of cooked pork and shrimp on one half of the banh xeo. Add a spoonful of the
    sautéed onion and bean sprouts on top. Fold the other half of the
    banh xeo over the filling and slide the banh xeo onto a plate.
  • Repeat with the remaining batter and filling to make more banh xeo.
  • Serve the banh xeo hot with lettuce leaves, fresh herbs, and dipping sauce on the side.
