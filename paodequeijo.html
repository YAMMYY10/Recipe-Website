PAO DE QUEIJO

INGREDIENTS



  • 2 cups sour cassava starch (or
    tapioca starch)
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup grated cheese (such as Parmesan)


INSTRUCTIONS



  • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • In a large bowl, combine the cassava starch and salt.
  • In a separate bowl, whisk together the vegetable oil, milk, and eggs.
  • Add the wet ingredients to the cassava starch mixture and stir until a dough forms.
  • Stir in the grated cheese until evenly distributed.
  • Roll the dough into small balls, about 1 inch in diameter, and place on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  • Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the Pao de Queijo are lightly golden and puffed up.
  • Serve warm and enjoy!
