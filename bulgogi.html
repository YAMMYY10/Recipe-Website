BULGOGI

INGREDIENTS



  • 1 pound of beef sirloin, sliced thinly
  • 1/2 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons of rice wine
  • 1 tablespoon of minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon of minced ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 4-5 green onions, sliced into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame seeds


INSTRUCTIONS



  • In a bowl, mix together soy sauce, brown sugar, sesame oil, rice wine, garlic, ginger, and black pepper.
  • Add sliced beef to the marinade, and let it marinate for at least 30 minutes (or up to overnight) in
    the refrigerator.
  • Heat a large skillet or grill over high heat. Add the thinly sliced onions to the skillet, and stir-fry for
    1-2 minutes.
  • Add the marinated beef to the skillet, and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the beef is cooked through.
  • Add the green onions, and stir-fry for an additional 1-2 minutes until the green onions are wilted.
  • Sprinkle sesame seeds on top of the bulgogi, and serve hot with steamed rice and your favorite
    side dishes.
