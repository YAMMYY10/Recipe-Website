INDONESIAN CUISINE


Nasi Goreng

This is a classic Indonesian fried rice dish that's made with rice, eggs, vegetables, and spices. It's often served with chicken, beef, or shrimp.




Satay

This is a popular Indonesian street food made with skewered meat, usually chicken or beef, that's marinated in a spicy sauce and grilled over charcoal. It's typically served with peanut sauce and a side of rice.




Soto Ayam

This is a traditional chicken soup that's flavored with lemongrass, turmeric, and other spices. It's often served with noodles, hard-boiled eggs, and crispy fried onions.




Rendang

This is a spicy beef curry that's slow-cooked with coconut milk, lemongrass, and other spices until the meat is tender and the sauce has thickened. It's a popular dish in the West Sumatra region.




Gado-gado

This is a vegetable salad that's made with boiled potatoes, boiled eggs, tofu, tempeh, and a variety of fresh vegetables. It's typically served with a peanut sauce dressing and crispy shrimp crackers.
