DOSA

INGREDIENTS



    For the Dosa batter:

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 cup urad dal (black gram lentil)
  • 1 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

    • For the Masala Filling:

  • 2-3 potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • A pinch of asafoetida
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste


INSTRUCTIONS



    For the Masala Filling:

  • Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and asafoetida. Let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions and green chili and saut√© till the onions turn translucent.
  • Add turmeric powder and mashed potatoes, and mix well.
  • Add salt to taste and mix well. Keep aside.

    • For making the Masala Dosa:

  • Heat a non-stick pan or a griddle over medium flame.
  • Grease the pan with a little oil.
  • Pour a ladleful of the dosa batter onto the pan and spread it in a circular motion to make a thin dosa.
  • Drizzle some oil around the edges and cook till the bottom is golden brown.
  • Spread the masala filling on one half of the dosa and fold the other half over it.
  • Cook for another minute and then serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
  • Enjoy your delicious Indian Masala Dosa!
