KEBAB

INGREDIENTS



  • 1 pound ground beef or lamb
  • 1 onion, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Wooden skewers


INSTRUCTIONS



  • In a large bowl, combine the ground beef or lamb with the grated onion, minced garlic, tomato paste, red pepper flakes, cumin, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are thoroughly combined.
  • Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let the mixture rest in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour.
  • Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  • Divide the meat mixture into 8-10 equal portions.
  • Take a wooden skewer and flatten a portion of the meat mixture around it, molding it into a long, thin sausage shape.
  • Repeat with the remaining portions of the meat mixture and wooden skewers.
  • Grill the kebabs for about 5-6 minutes on each side, or until they are cooked through and have a nice charred exterior.
  • Serve hot with rice, salad, and pita bread.
