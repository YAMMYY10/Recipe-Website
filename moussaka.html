MOUSSAKA

INGREDIENTS



  • 2 large eggplants
  • 1 lb ground lamb
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten


INSTRUCTIONS



  • Slice eggplants into 1/4 inch rounds, salt them and let them sit for 30 minutes. Then rinse them and pat them dry with paper towels.
  • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the ground lamb until browned. Remove the lamb and set aside.
  • In the same skillet, cook the onion and garlic until soft. Add the tomato paste, diced tomatoes, cinnamon, nutmeg, oregano, salt and pepper, and simmer for 10 minutes.
  • Meanwhile, in a separate skillet, fry the eggplant rounds in oil until golden brown. Drain them on paper towels.
  • In a small saucepan, melt the butter over low heat. Add the flour and whisk until smooth. Gradually add the milk, whisking constantly, until the mixture thickens. Remove from heat and whisk in the beaten eggs.
  • In a baking dish, layer half of the eggplant rounds, followed by half of the meat mixture. Repeat the layers.
  • Pour the egg and milk mixture over the top of the layers, then sprinkle the breadcrumbs and Parmesan cheese over the top.
  • Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the dish is heated through.
  • Remove from the oven and let cool for 10-15 minutes before serving. Enjoy your delicious Greek Moussaka!
