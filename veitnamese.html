VEITNAMESE CUISINE


Banh Mi

A Vietnamese sandwich made with a crispy French baguette that is filled with various ingredients, such as grilled pork, pate, pickled vegetables, and cilantro. It is a popular street food in Vietnam and around the world.




Pho

A famous Vietnamese noodle soup made with a flavorful beef or chicken broth, rice noodles, and a variety of herbs and vegetables. It is usually served with sliced beef or chicken and garnished with bean sprouts, basil leaves, lime, and chili sauce.




Cao Lau

A regional specialty of Hoi An, Cao Lau is a noodle dish made with thick, chewy noodles, slices of pork, bean sprouts, and greens, served in a savory broth. It is a unique dish that is only found in the town of Hoi An.




Banh Xeo

A crispy and savory Vietnamese pancake made with rice flour, turmeric, coconut milk, and filled with shrimp, pork, bean sprouts, and green onions. It is often served with lettuce, herbs, and dipping sauce.




Goi Cuon

Also known as Vietnamese fresh spring rolls, goi cuon is a refreshing and healthy dish made with rice paper wrappers filled with shrimp, pork, or tofu, and a variety of fresh herbs and vegetables, such as lettuce, mint, and bean sprouts. It is usually served with a peanut dipping sauce.
