EMPANADAS

INGREDIENTS



    For the dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter, cold
    and cubed
  • 1/2 cup cold water

    • For the filling:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup raisins
  • 1/4 cup green olives, pitted and chopped
  • 1/4 cup beef broth or water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)


INSTRUCTIONS



  • For the dough, mix together the flour and salt in a large bowl.
  • Add the cubed butter and mix with a pastry cutter or your fingers until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  • Add the cold water and mix until a dough forms.
  • Knead the dough on a floured surface for a few minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  • Divide the dough into 12 equal pieces and roll each piece into a ball.
  • Cover the dough balls with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  • For the filling, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  • Add the ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up with a wooden spoon.
  • Add the onion, red bell pepper, and garlic to the skillet and cook until the vegetables are soft.
  • Add the cumin, paprika, oregano, and red pepper flakes and stir to combine.
  • Stir in the raisins, green olives, and beef broth or water.
  • Simmer the mixture for 5-10 minutes, until the liquid has evaporated and the filling is thick.
  • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  • Roll out each dough ball on a floured surface to about 1/8 inch thickness.
  • Place a spoonful of the filling in the center of each dough round.
  • Brush the edges of the dough with water and fold the dough over to enclose the filling.
  • Use a fork to press the edges of the dough together and seal the empanadas.
  • Place the empanadas on the prepared baking sheet.
  • Brush the tops of the empanadas with the beaten egg.
  • Enjoy your deliciously baked Empanadas!
