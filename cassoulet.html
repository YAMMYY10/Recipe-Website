CASSOULET

INGREDIENTS



  • 500g white beans (haricot beans or cannellini beans)
  • 1kg mixed meats (pork belly, pork shoulder, lamb, sausages)
  • 200g bacon, diced
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 sprig thyme
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon flour
  • 1 liter chicken or vegetable stock
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Olive oil
  • Breadcrumbs


INSTRUCTIONS



  • Soak the white beans in cold water overnight, then drain and rinse them.
  • Preheat the oven to 160Â°C.
  • Heat some olive oil in a large Dutch oven or casserole dish over medium-high heat.
  • Brown the mixed meats on all sides in the Dutch oven, then remove them and set them aside.
  • In the same Dutch oven, cook the bacon until crispy, then remove it and set it aside.
  • Add the onions, carrots, and garlic to the Dutch oven and cook until softened.
  • Sprinkle the flour over the vegetables and stir until everything is coated.
  • Add the tomato paste, bay leaves, thyme, and stock to the Dutch oven, then return the mixed meats and bacon to the pot.
  • Bring everything to a simmer, then cover the Dutch oven and transfer it to the oven.
  • Let everything cook for 2-3 hours, or until the beans are tender and the meats are falling off the bone.
  • Remove the Dutch oven from the oven and adjust the seasoning to taste with salt and pepper.
  • Sprinkle breadcrumbs over the top of the cassoulet and return it to the oven, uncovered, for another 15-20
    minutes, or until the breadcrumbs are golden and crispy.
  • Serve hot, garnished with chopped parsley or thyme.
  • Enjoy your delicious cassoulet!
