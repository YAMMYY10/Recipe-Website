PATATAS BRAVAS

INGREDIENTS



  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and cut
    into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 cup of tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sherry vinegar
  • 1/4 cup of mayonnaise


INSTRUCTIONS



  • Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  • Toss the potato pieces with 2 tablespoons of olive oil and spread them out on a baking sheet.
  • Bake the potatoes in the preheated oven for 20-25
    minutes, or until they are golden and crispy.
  • While the potatoes are cooking, make the bravas sauce by heating the remaining 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a small saucepan over medium heat.
  • Add the minced garlic, smoked paprika, cayenne pepper, and salt to the saucepan and cook for 1-2 minutes,
    or until fragrant.
  • Add the tomato sauce and sherry vinegar to the saucepan and stir well.
  • Simmer the bravas sauce for 5-10 minutes, or until it has thickened slightly.
  • Remove the bravas sauce from the heat and set it aside.
  • In a separate bowl, whisk the mayonnaise with a tablespoon of water until it becomes smooth and pourable.
  • To serve, place the cooked potatoes on a plate and drizzle them with the bravas sauce and the
    mayonnaise mixture.
  • Enjoy!
