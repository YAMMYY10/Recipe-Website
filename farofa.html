FAROFA

INGREDIENTS



  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups cassava flour (also called manioc or yuca flour)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley, for garnish (optional)


INSTRUCTIONS



  • Melt the butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
  • Add the chopped onion and garlic and saut√© until the onion is translucent and the garlic is fragrant,
    about 5 minutes.
  • Add the cassava flour to the skillet and stir to combine with the onion mixture.
  • Cook the mixture, stirring frequently, for 5-10 minutes, or until the flour is lightly toasted and fragrant.
  • Season the farofa with salt and black pepper to taste.
  • Transfer the farofa to a serving bowl and sprinkle with chopped fresh parsley, if desired.
  • Serve the farofa as a side dish with your favorite Brazilian meals, such as feijoada or grilled meats.
