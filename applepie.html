APPLE PIE

INGREDIENTS



  • 6 cups peeled and thinly sliced apples (12-14 medium apples)
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, cut into small pieces
  • 2 prepared pie crusts


INSTRUCTIONS



  • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • In a large bowl, combine the sliced apples, sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt. Stir well to coat the apples.
  • Fit one prepared pie crust into a 9-inch (23-cm) pie dish. Add the apple mixture to the pie crust and dot with the pieces of butter.
  • Cover the apple mixture with the second prepared pie crust. Trim any excess dough and crimp the edges of the
    pie crusts together to seal.
  • Cut a few slits in the top of the pie crust to allow steam to escape.
  • Bake the pie for 45-55 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.
  • Remove the pie from the oven and allow it to cool on a wire rack for at least 30 minutes before serving.
