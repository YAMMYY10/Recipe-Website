PAPOUTSAKIA

INGREDIENTS



  • 4 medium eggplants
  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup red wine
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish


INSTRUCTIONS



  • Preheat the oven to 375Â°F (190Â°C).
  • Cut the eggplants in half lengthwise, and scoop out the flesh with a spoon, leaving about 1/2 inch of flesh around the skin. Reserve the eggplant flesh in a bowl.
  • Brush the eggplant skins with olive oil, and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake for 20 minutes or until the skins are lightly browned and tender.
  • Meanwhile, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and sautÃ© for 2-3 minutes until softened.
  • Add the ground beef, and cook until browned, breaking up any lumps with a spoon.
  • Add the reserved eggplant flesh, tomato sauce, red wine, oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine, and simmer for 10-15 minutes until the sauce has thickened.
  • Fill each eggplant skin with the beef mixture, and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
  • Return the eggplants to the oven, and bake for another 20-25 minutes until the cheese is melted and the eggplants are tender.
  • Garnish with fresh parsley, and serve hot.
