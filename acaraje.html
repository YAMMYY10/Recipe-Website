ACARAJE

INGREDIENTS



  • 1 pound black-eyed peas, soaked overnight
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • Dried shrimp, chopped
  • Hot sauce or salsa, for serving


INSTRUCTIONS



  • Drain and rinse the soaked black-eyed peas. Transfer them to a food processor and pulse until they
    form a coarse paste.
  • Add the chopped onion, garlic, salt, and cayenne pepper to the food processor and pulse until well combined.
  • Using your hands, form the mixture into small patties, about 2-3 inches in diameter.
  • In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
  • Carefully add the patties to the hot oil, a few at a time, and fry until golden brown on both sides, about
    3-4 minutes per side.
  • Remove the Acarajé from the oil using a slotted spoon and drain on a paper towel-lined plate.
  • Split the Acarajé in half and fill each half with chopped dried shrimp and hot sauce or salsa, if desired.
  • Serve hot and enjoy!
