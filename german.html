GERMAN CUISINE


Schnitzel

This is a thin, breaded cutlet of veal, pork, or chicken that is fried until it's crispy and golden brown. It's typically served with a side of potatoes and a salad.




Sauerbraten

This is a pot roast that's marinated in vinegar, wine, and spices for several days before it's cooked. It's typically served with a side of potato dumplings or spaetzle.




Bratwurst

This is a type of German sausage that's made from pork, beef, or veal. It's grilled or fried and served with sauerkraut, mustard, and a side of bread or potatoes.




Kartoffelsuppe

This is a potato soup that's made with diced potatoes, leeks, onions, and bacon. It's typically served with a side of bread or rolls.




Kartoffelpuffer

Also known as potato pancakes or potato fritters, are a popular German dish made from grated potatoes, eggs, flour, and spices. The mixture is then fried in oil until it's crispy and golden brown.
