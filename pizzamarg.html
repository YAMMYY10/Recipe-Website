PIZZA MARGHERITA

INGREDIENTS



  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 8 oz. fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt
  • Olive oil


INSTRUCTIONS



  • Preheat the oven to 450Â°F.
  • On a lightly floured surface, roll out the pizza dough into a round shape.Place the dough on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  • Place the dough on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  • Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the pizza dough, leaving a little room around the edges.
  • Add the slices of fresh mozzarella cheese on top of the tomato sauce.
  • Sprinkle a pinch of salt over the cheese.
  • Drizzle a little bit of olive oil over the pizza.
  • Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  • Remove the pizza from the oven and top with fresh basil leaves.
  • Slice and serve hot.
