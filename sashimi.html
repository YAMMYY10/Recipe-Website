SASHIMI

INGREDIENTS



  • Fresh sashimi-grade fish (e.g. tuna, salmon, yellowtail)
  • Soy sauce
  • Wasabi
  • Pickled ginger


INSTRUCTIONS



  • Buy fresh sashimi-grade fish from a reputable fishmonger or Japanese market.
  • Rinse the fish in cold water and pat dry with paper towels.
  • Cut the fish into thin slices using a sharp knife, making sure to remove any bones or skin.
  • Arrange the fish slices on a plate, leaving some space between each piece.
  • Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger on the side.
  • To eat, dip the fish into the soy sauce and add a small amount of wasabi to taste. Eat with a piece of pickled ginger to cleanse the palate between bites.
  • Enjoy your homemade Japanese sashimi!
