COZIDO A PORTUGUESA

INGREDIENTS



  • 500g beef shank, cut into chunks
  • 500g pork belly, cut into chunks
  • 1 Portuguese chouriço sausage, sliced
  • 1 black pudding sausage, sliced
  • 1 white pudding sausage, sliced
  • 1 cabbage, chopped into large pieces
  • 4 medium carrots, peeled and cut
    into chunks
  • 4 medium potatoes, peeled and cut
    into chunks
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Water


INSTRUCTIONS



  • In a large pot, sauté the onion and garlic until fragrant.
  • Add the beef and pork to the pot and brown on all sides.
  • Add enough water to cover the meat, along with the bay leaves, paprika, cumin, and salt and pepper.
  • Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer for 1 hour.
  • Add the chouriço, black pudding, and white pudding to the pot and simmer for another 30 minutes.
  • Add the cabbage, carrots, and potatoes and simmer for another 30 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.
  • Taste and adjust seasoning as necessary.
  • Serve hot with some crusty bread on the side.
