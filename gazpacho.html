GAZPACHO

INGREDIENTS



  • 4 large ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 small red onion, chopped
  • 1 small cucumber, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 1/4 cup of extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of sherry vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 cups of cold water
  • Croutons and chopped parsley for garnish (optional)


INSTRUCTIONS



  • In a blender or food processor, combine the chopped tomatoes, red onion, cucumber, red bell pepper, and garlic.
  • Add the olive oil, sherry vinegar, salt, and black pepper to the blender and blend until the mixture is smooth.
  • Add the cold water to the blender and blend again until the gazpacho reaches your desired consistency.
  • Taste and adjust the seasoning as necessary.
  • Chill the gazpacho in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour before serving.
  • Garnish with croutons and chopped parsley, if desired.
  • Serve and enjoy!
