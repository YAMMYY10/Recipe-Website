COQ AU VIN

INGREDIENTS



  • 4 chicken legs or 1 whole chicken, cut
    into pieces
  • 200g bacon, chopped
  • 250g mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bouquet garni (thyme, bay leaf,
    parsley)
  • 750ml red wine
  • 250ml chicken stock
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Olive oil
  • Butter


INSTRUCTIONS



  • Heat some olive oil and butter in a large Dutch oven or casserole dish over medium-high heat.
  • Season the chicken with salt and pepper, then brown the pieces on all sides in the Dutch oven. Remove the
    chicken and set it aside.
  • In the same Dutch oven, cook the bacon until crispy, then remove it and set it aside.
  • Add the onions, carrots, and mushrooms to the Dutch oven and cook until softened.
  • Add the garlic and tomato paste and cook for another minute.
  • Sprinkle the flour over the vegetables and stir until everything is coated.
  • Add the red wine, chicken stock, and bouquet garni to the Dutch oven, then return the chicken and bacon to the pot.
  • Bring everything to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low and cover the Dutch oven. Let everything cook for
    1-1.5 hours, or until the chicken is tender and cooked through.
  • Remove the bouquet garni from the Dutch oven and adjust the seasoning to taste with salt and pepper.
  • Serve hot, garnished with chopped parsley or thyme, and with a side of crusty bread or mashed potatoes.
  • Enjoy your delicious coq au vin!
