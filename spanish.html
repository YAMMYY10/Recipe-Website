SPANISH CUISINE


Paella

Paella is a traditional Spanish rice dish that typically includes saffron, chicken, rabbit, seafood, and vegetables such as bell peppers, onions, and tomatoes.




Gazpacho

Gazpacho is a cold Spanish soup typically made with raw, blended vegetables, such as tomatoes, cucumbers, bell peppers, and onions, along with garlic, bread, olive oil, vinegar, and salt.




Tortilla Española

Tortilla Española, also known as tortilla de patatas, is a traditional Spanish dish that is similar to an omelette or a frittata. It is typically made with potatoes, onions, and eggs, and is a staple dish in Spanish cuisine.




Patatas bravas

Patatas bravas is a popular Spanish tapa that consists of crispy fried potatoes served with a spicy tomato sauce. In addition to the spicy tomato sauce, patatas bravas is also served with a creamy aioli sauce, which is made with garlic, olive oil, and egg yolks.




Pisto

Pisto is a traditional Spanish dish similar to ratatouille or a vegetable stew made by sautéing onions, peppers, and tomatoes, and then adding other vegetables like zucchini, eggplant, and potatoes.
