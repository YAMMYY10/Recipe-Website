MATAMBRE ARROLLADO

INGREDIENTS



  • 1 large flank steak
  • 1/2 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tbsp chopped garlic
  • 2 tbsp dried oregano
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper


INSTRUCTIONS



  • Place the flank steak in a large resealable plastic bag.
  • In a bowl, whisk together the red wine vinegar, olive oil, onion, parsley, garlic, oregano, paprika,
    cumin, salt, and black pepper.
  • Pour the marinade into the plastic bag with the flank steak and seal the bag, removing as much air
    as possible.
  • Marinate the flank steak in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  • Preheat your grill to high heat.
  • Remove the flank steak from the marinade and discard the marinade.
  • Grill the flank steak for about 8-10 minutes per side for medium-rare or until desired doneness.
  • Let the flank steak rest for 5-10 minutes before slicing against the grain into thin slices.
  • Serve the metambre hot with your favorite sides.
