MANTI

INGREDIENTS



  • 500g ground beef or lamb
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste


INSTRUCTIONS



  • In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, eggs, and water. Knead the dough until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  • Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  • In a separate bowl, mix together the ground beef or lamb, chopped onion, minced garlic, salt, and black pepper.
  • Roll out the dough on a floured surface to a thickness of about 1/8 inch.
  • Cut the dough into small squares, about 2 inches in size.
  • Place a small spoonful of the meat mixture in the center of each dough square.
  • Fold the dough in half to create a triangle, and then pinch the edges together to seal the filling inside.
  • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  • Add the manti to the boiling water and cook for 10-15 minutes, or until they float to the surface.
  • In a small pan, melt the butter and olive oil together.
  • Serve the cooked manti with a dollop of plain yogurt and drizzle with the butter and olive oil mixture.
