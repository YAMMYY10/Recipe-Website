CHEESEBURGER

INGREDIENTS



  • 1/4 pound 85% lean ground beef
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 slice American cheese
  • 1 hamburger bun
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion, and condiments of your choice


INSTRUCTIONS



  • Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  • Divide the ground beef into 2 equal portions and shape each into a patty. Season each patty with salt and pepper.
  • Grill the patties for about 3-4 minutes per side, or until cooked to your desired level of doneness.
  • Add the cheese to one of the patties during the last minute of cooking and allow it to melt.
  • Toast the hamburger bun on the grill for about 1 minute, or until lightly browned.
  • Assemble the burger by placing the lettuce, tomato, and onion on the bottom half of the bun, followed by the
    patty with melted cheese, and then any condiments of your choice. Top with the other half of the bun.
  • Enjoy your delicious cheeseburger.
