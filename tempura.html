TEMPURA

INGREDIENTS



  • Assorted vegetables and/or seafood (e.g. shrimp,squid, sweet potato, eggplant, green beans)
  • Tempura flour
  • Ice water
  • Soy sauce
  • Mirin
  • Dashi (Japanese soup stock)
  • Grated daikon radish (optional)
  • Grated ginger (optional)


INSTRUCTIONS



  • Cut the vegetables and/or seafood into bite-sized pieces.
  • In a mixing bowl, combine the tempura flour and ice water until the batter is smooth and slightly thick. Don't overmix.
  • Heat a pot or deep fryer with vegetable oil to 350°F (180°C).
  • Dip the vegetables and/or seafood into the batter and shake off any excess.
  • Fry the pieces in the hot oil until golden brown, turning once or twice.
  • Drain the tempura on paper towels to remove any excess oil.
  • Serve the tempura with a dipping sauce made from soy sauce, mirin, and dashi. You can also add grated daikon radish and/or ginger to the sauce for extra flavor.
  • Enjoy your homemade Japanese tempura!
