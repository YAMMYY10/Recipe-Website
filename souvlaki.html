SOUVLAKI

INGREDIENTS



  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts or pork tenderloin, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Wooden skewers


INSTRUCTIONS



  • Soak wooden skewers in water for at least 30 minutes.
  • In a bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, red wine vinegar, garlic, oregano, salt, and black pepper.
  • Add the chicken or pork to the bowl and toss to coat well. Cover and marinate in the fridge for at least 30 minutes or up to 4 hours.
  • Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  • Thread the chicken or pork onto skewers, leaving a little space between each piece.
  • Grill the skewers, turning occasionally, until the chicken or pork is cooked through and slightly charred, about 10-12 minutes.
  • Serve hot with tzatziki sauce and pita bread, if desired. Enjoy!
