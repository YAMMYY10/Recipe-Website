ASADO

INGREDIENTS



  • 1 kg beef ribs (asado cut)
  • 1 kg beef flank steak (vacio)
  • 1 kg beef short ribs (costilla)
  • 1 kg beef sausage (chorizo)
  • 1 kg sweetbread (mollejas)
  • 1 kg beef kidney (ri√±on)
  • 2 kg firewood or charcoal
  • Coarse salt (sal gruesa)
  • Chimichurri sauce (optional)


INSTRUCTIONS



  • Start the fire with the firewood or charcoal.
  • Wait until the coals are hot and have a layer of white ash on top.
  • Season the meats generously with coarse salt.
  • Place the beef ribs, flank steak, and short ribs on the grill, bone side down.
  • Cook for about 20-30 minutes on each side, until they are nicely browned and cooked to your liking.
  • Add the beef sausage, sweetbread, and kidney to the grill.
  • Cook for about 10-15 minutes on each side, until they are cooked through.
  • Remove the meats from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before slicing.
  • Serve with chimichurri sauce on the side, if desired.
