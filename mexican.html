MEXICAN CUISINE


Chilaquiles

Chilaquiles is a traditional Mexican breakfast dish that consists of lightly fried corn tortillas that are cut into quarters and served with a variety of toppings. The tortillas are typically simmered in a tomato-based sauce or salsa until they are soft, but still retain their texture.




Tacos

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish consisting of a tortilla filled with various ingredients, such as meat, vegetables, cheese, salsa, and guacamole. The tortilla can be made from either corn or wheat flour and is usually soft and pliable. There are numerous tacos including carne asada (grilled beef), al pastor (marinated pork), pollo (chicken), vegetarian, and fish tacos.




Mole Poblano

Mole poblano is a traditional Mexican sauce that is often served with poultry, such as chicken or turkey. The sauce is made from a variety of ingredients, including chili peppers, nuts, seeds, spices, and chocolate.




Tamales

Tamales are a traditional Mexican dish made from masa (a dough made from corn) that is typically filled with meat, cheese, vegetables, or chilies, and then steamed or boiled in a corn husk.




Chiles en nogada

The dish consists of poblano peppers that are stuffed with a mixture of ground meat, fruits, and spices, and then topped with a walnut cream sauce and pomegranate seeds. Chiles en nogada is a dish that is rich in flavor and has a unique combination of sweet and savory ingredients.
