CEVICHE

INGREDIENTS



  • 1 lb of fresh raw fish (sea bass or tilapia)
  • 1 red onion
  • 1-2 hot peppers (aji amarillo or habanero)
  • 1 cup of fresh lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Corn on the cob (optional)
  • Sweet potato (optional)


INSTRUCTIONS



  • Cut the fish into bite-size pieces and place it in a large bowl.
  • Thinly slice the red onion and hot peppers, and add them to the bowl with the fish.
  • Pour the lime juice over the fish mixture and mix well.
  • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 20 minutes or until the fish is "cooked" by the acid from the lime juice.
  • Before serving, garnish with fresh cilantro.
  • Serve with boiled corn on the cob and sliced sweet potato on the side, if desired.
  • Enjoy your delicious Peruvian ceviche!
