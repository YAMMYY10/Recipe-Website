LASAGNE ALLA BOLOGNESE

INGREDIENTS



  • lb. lasagne noodles
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup red wine
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 celery stalk, chopped
  • 1 carrot, chopped
  • 2 tbsp. olive oil
  • 2 tbsp. butter
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • salt and pepper to taste


INSTRUCTIONS



  • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • Cook lasagne noodles according to package directions.
  • In a large skillet, heat olive oil and butter over medium heat. Add onion, celery, and carrot, and cook until softened.
  • Add ground beef and garlic to the skillet, and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  • Add tomato sauce and red wine to the skillet, and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  • In a separate bowl, mix together ricotta cheese, eggs, and Parmesan cheese.
  • Spread a layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Cover with a layer of lasagne noodles, then a layer of the ricotta mixture.
  • Repeat layers, ending with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  • Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  • Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  • Let cool for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
