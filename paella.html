PAELLA

INGREDIENTS



  • 2 cups of bomba rice
  • 1/2 cup of olive oil
  • 4 chicken thighs, bone-in and skin-on
  • 8-10 large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 3 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of saffron threads
  • 4 cups of chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper
  • Lemon wedges and chopped parsley for garnish


INSTRUCTIONS



  • Heat the olive oil in a paella pan over medium-high heat.
  • Season the chicken with salt and pepper, then add it to the pan and cook until browned on both sides, about 5-7 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the pan and set it aside.
  • Add the onion and red bell pepper to the pan and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  • Add the garlic and cook for another minute.
  • Add the chopped tomatoes, smoked paprika, and saffron threads and stir well.
  • Add the rice to the pan and stir it into the tomato mixture until it is evenly coated.
  • Add the chicken broth to the pan and stir everything together.
  • Return the chicken to the pan and nestle it into the rice mixture.
  • Reduce the heat to medium-low and let the paella simmer for about 20 minutes.
  • After 20 minutes, add the shrimp to the pan and cook for another 5-7 minutes until the shrimp is cooked through and the rice is tender.
  • Remove the paella from the heat and let it rest for a few minutes.
  • Garnish with lemon wedges and chopped parsley before serving. Enjoy!
