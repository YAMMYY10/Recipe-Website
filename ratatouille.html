RATATOUILLE

INGREDIENTS



  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 eggplant, chopped into small cubes
  • 2 zucchinis, chopped into small cubes
  • 2 bell peppers, chopped into small cubes
  • 4 ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh rosemary
  • Salt and pepper, to taste


INSTRUCTIONS



  • Heat some olive oil in a large skillet or Dutch oven over medium-high heat.
  • Add the onions and garlic to the skillet and cook until softened.
  • Add the eggplant, zucchinis, and bell peppers to the skillet and cook until they start to brown.
  • Add the tomatoes, tomato paste, thyme, and rosemary to the skillet, and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Stir everything together and let the mixture cook for 15-20 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
  • Adjust the seasoning to taste, if needed.
  • Serve hot, garnished with chopped parsley or basil, with crusty bread or rice on the side.
  • Enjoy your delicious ratatouille!
