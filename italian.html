ITALIAN CUISINE


Lasagne alla Bolognese

Lasagne alla Bolognese is a traditional Italian dish that is made with layers of pasta, a rich meat sauce, and creamy bechamel sauce.




Risotto

Risotto is a traditional Italian dish made with Arborio or Carnaroli rice, broth, butter, and Parmesan cheese.




Pizza Margherita

Pizza Margherita is a classic pizza that originated in Naples, Italy. It is a simple pizza that is made with only a few high-quality ingredients.




Fettuccine al Pomodoro

Fettuccine al Pomodoro is a simple yet delicious Italian pasta dish that consists of fettuccine noodles tossed with a tomato-based sauce.




Gnocchi di Patate

Gnocchi di Patate are small potato dumplings made from a mixture of boiled potatoes, flour, and sometimes eggs or cheese.
