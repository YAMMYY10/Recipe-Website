LAHMACUN

INGREDIENTS



  • 1 lb ground lamb or beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp red pepper paste
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 lb pizza dough
  • Lemon wedges and fresh parsley for serving.


INSTRUCTIONS



  • Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C).
  • In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground lamb or beef, chopped onion, minced garlic, chopped tomatoes,
    tomato paste, red pepper paste, chopped parsley and mint, paprika, cumin, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until
    all ingredients are fully combined.
  • Divide the pizza dough into 8 equal pieces and roll each piece into a thin oval shape, about 8 inches long and 5 inches wide.
  • Spread a thin layer of the meat mixture over each piece of dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  • Place the lahmacun on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for 8-10 minutes, or until the crust
    is golden brown and crispy.
  • Remove the lahmacun from the oven and serve hot with fresh parsley and lemon wedges.
  • Enjoy your delicious Turkish Lahmacun!
